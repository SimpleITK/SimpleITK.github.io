Detailed Description

Morphological closing by attributes.

An attribute closing removes blobs according to criteria such as area. When applied to grayscale images they have the effect of filling valleys (regions darker than their surroundings) based on area while leaving the rest of the image unchanged.

This code was contributed in the Insight Journal paper

"Grayscale morphological attribute operations" by Beare R. https://hdl.handle.net/1926/1316 https://www.insight-journal.org/browse/publication/203

Author
Richard Beare. Department of Medicine, Monash University, Melbourne, Australia.
See also
itk::simple::AreaClosing for the procedural interface
itk::AreaClosingImageFilter for the Doxygen on the original ITK class.
