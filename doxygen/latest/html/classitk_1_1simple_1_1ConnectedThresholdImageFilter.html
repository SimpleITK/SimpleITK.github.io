Detailed Description

Label pixels that are connected to a seed and lie within a range of values.

ConnectedThresholdImageFilter labels pixels with ReplaceValue that are connected to an initial Seed AND lie within a Lower and Upper threshold range.

See also
itk::simple::ConnectedThreshold for the procedural interface
itk::ConnectedThresholdImageFilter for the Doxygen on the original ITK class.
Examples
ITKIntegration/ITKIntegration.cxx, and Segmentation/ConnectedThresholdImageFilter.cxx.
